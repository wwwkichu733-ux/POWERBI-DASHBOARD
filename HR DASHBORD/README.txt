HR Analysis Power BI Dashboard

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI.
The dashboard helps HR teams and management understand employee attrition, demographic distribution, job satisfaction, and workforce trends. It offers a comprehensive view of employee behavior and organizational health.
